Are you bored of city life and want to take a break?

Well, then you're in the right place! Stardew Valley is a farming simulator game about a farmer(you) who is bored of working 9-5 constantly and stressed by the overwhelming city life. One day you just decide to up and go - to your beloved Grandpa's farm! You will be designing this farm from stratch, meet new people and discover their stories.


You can build relationships with the townsfolk, discover about their life and maybe little secrets, even! Who knows, maybe your soulmate is waiting for you in the Stardew Valley right now?
